About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Laboratoire de Physique Nucléaire et des Hautes Energies (LPNHE) based on Sorbonne Université. I am interested mainly in provide improvements on electron and photon trigger and reconstruction, and analyis developments for Long Lived Axion Like Particles, with colimated photons in final states.

I am funded by Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS) through a 2-year ANR (Agence Nationale de la Recherche) postdoctoral program.

Research Interests

Fast selection of rare particles in high energy physics experiments.

Efficient feature extraction for electron and photon signatures in the ATLAS Experiment.

Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN) for electron and photon identification.

Machine learning theory and signal processing.
