About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Laboratoire de Physique Nucléaire et des Hautes Energies (LPNHE) based on Sorbonne Université. Working with ATLAS Experiment at CERN I am interested in improvements on electron and photon trigger and reconstruction, and physics analyis for Long Lived Axion Like Particles (H -> Za channel), with closed-by photons in final states.

I am funded by Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS) through a 2-year ANR (Agence Nationale de la Recherche) postdoctoral program.

Research Interests

Feature extraction algorithms for electron and photon energy deposits in the ATLAS Experiment.

Computational Intelligence applied for electron and photon identification.

Machine learning theory and signal processing.
