I am an Electronic Engineer working with experimental particle physics at the ATLAS experiment of the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at CERN. Experienced computational systems based on machine learning and signal processing, especially for online efficient selection of electrons and photons signatures. I am mainly interested in bioinspired algorithms , statistical signal processing , signature feature extraction in High Energy Physics.
